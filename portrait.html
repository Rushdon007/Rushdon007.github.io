Portraits:


I aim to give emphasis on the face of the person because this will also be the focus I aim to give emphasis on the face of the person because this will also be the focus or the emphasis of the photograph. This does not mean, however, that the people body or even the background will no longer be included. Under portrait photography, these can still be included in the photo by the portrait photographer but again, the focus or the emphasis should be on the people face, facial expression and even distinct facial features.
